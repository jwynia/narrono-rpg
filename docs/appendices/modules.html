Narrono Game Manual

Adventure Modules

Adventure modules are pre-built scenarios designed to help players and Game Facilitators quickly begin playing with minimal preparation. Each module provides a complete package with setting information, a structured adventure framework, and pre-generated characters.

Available Modules

Shadow Court

A complete adventure set in a world where ancient noble families secretly wield supernatural powers. Players take on the roles of individuals who discover and become entangled in the supernatural politics of Ravenshire, where shadow magic and family secrets drive the narrative.

Features:

  • Gothic urban fantasy setting with supernatural elements
  • Mystery-driven narrative with political intrigue
  • Character archetypes including nobles, investigators, and reformed criminals
  • Ready-to-play pre-generated characters with complete traits, assets, and relationships

Frontier Clans

An adventure where rival clans vie for territory in a harsh post-collapse frontier. Players take on the roles of clan members navigating the challenges of survival, complex alliances, ancient traditions, and territorial conflicts in a reclaimed wilderness where the remnants of old-world technology blend with new ways of life.

Features:

  • Post-apocalyptic frontier setting with minimal supernatural elements
  • Survival-focused narrative with clan politics and resource conflicts
  • Character archetypes including scouts, mechanics, diplomats, and knowledge keepers
  • Mixed technology level where scavenged artifacts meet handcrafted innovations

Corporate Wars

An adventure set in a world of mega-corporations competing for global dominance. Players take on the roles of corporate operatives, executives, or outsiders caught in the ruthless competition between powerful companies that control vast resources and influence global policy.

Features:

  • Near-future setting with advanced technology but no supernatural elements
  • Corporate intrigue with economic and political power struggles
  • Character archetypes including security specialists, researchers, negotiators, and data experts
  • High-stakes conflicts where profit motives often clash with ethical considerations

How to Use These Modules

These modules are designed for quick play with minimal preparation:

  1. Read the Setting Document: Familiarize yourself with the world where the adventure takes place
  2. Review Pre-Generated Characters: Understand the character options and their connections to the story
  3. Follow the Adventure Framework: Use the structured scenes as a foundation for your narrative
  4. Adapt as Needed: Modify any elements to suit your group's preferences and play style

Each module contains everything you need for several sessions of play, and can be expanded into a longer campaign if desired.
