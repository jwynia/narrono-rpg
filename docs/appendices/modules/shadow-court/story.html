Shadow Court: Adventure Framework

This document provides a structured framework for running the Shadow Court adventure. It's designed to be flexible, allowing Game Facilitators to adapt it to their group's choices while maintaining a coherent narrative.

Adventure Structure

The adventure is organized into three acts, each containing several scenes. For each scene, you'll find:

  • Scene overview
  • Key NPCs
  • Important locations
  • Suggested challenges
  • Potential outcomes
  • Transition options

Act 1: Shadows Glimpsed

Scene 1: The Invitation

Setting: A social event in Ravenshire Overview: Characters receive invitations to an exclusive event hosted by a minor noble, where they witness something unusual that hints at shadow powers.

Key NPCs:

  • Lord Ambrose Harrington (Minor Noble Host)
  • Lady Eliza Webb (Observant Shadow Seer)
  • Sebastian Clarke (Keeper Observer)

Challenges:

  • Social navigation among Ravenshire's elite
  • Noticing subtle shadow manipulations
  • Reacting appropriately to unusual events

Potential Outcomes:

  • Characters notice shadow manipulation and become curious
  • Characters make important social connections
  • Characters attract unwanted attention from a Keeper

Scene 2: Strange Occurrences

Setting: Various locations around Ravenshire Overview: In the days following the event, characters notice strange phenomena and may begin investigating.

Key NPCs:

  • Detective Inspector Morris (Police Investigator)
  • Professor Eleanor Walsh (Shadow Researcher)
  • Thomas Reed (Nervous Witness)

Challenges:

  • Connecting seemingly unrelated incidents
  • Avoiding unwanted noble attention
  • Distinguishing ordinary events from shadow influence

Potential Outcomes:

  • Characters begin to understand the pattern of shadow activity
  • Characters make contact with helpful or dangerous NPCs
  • Characters become targets of shadow manipulation

Scene 3: The First Confrontation

Setting: An abandoned building in the Old Quarter Overview: Characters follow leads to an old building where they directly witness shadow powers in use and may experience them firsthand.

Key NPCs:

  • Marcus Webb (Reluctant Noble Scion)
  • Keeper Agents (Shadow Enforcers)
  • Clara Hayes (Investigative Reporter)

Challenges:

  • Evading or confronting shadow users
  • Processing the reality of supernatural powers
  • Making critical decisions about their involvement

Potential Outcomes:

  • Characters commit to investigating shadow powers
  • Characters form alliances with NPCs who can help them
  • Characters become known to the shadow community

Act 2: Shadows Revealed

Scene 4: Seeking Knowledge

Setting: The University and private libraries Overview: Characters search for information about shadow abilities and Ravenshire's noble families.

Key NPCs:

  • Professor Walsh (Shadow Researcher)
  • Archivist Holloway (Keeper-Adjacent Scholar)
  • Student Activist Group (Shadow-Unaware)

Challenges:

  • Finding restricted information
  • Distinguishing facts from myths
  • Avoiding surveillance by noble agents

Potential Outcomes:

  • Characters learn the basics of shadow powers
  • Characters discover the existence of the Shadow Pact
  • Characters find evidence linking nobles to historical events

Scene 5: Noble Approaches

Setting: Various noble estates Overview: One or more noble houses approach the characters, either to recruit, threaten, or eliminate them.

Key NPCs:

  • Lady Catherine Thornfield (Ambitious Noble)
  • Lord Richard Devereux (Cautious Noble)
  • Elena Blackwood (Noble Heir in Conflict)

Challenges:

  • Navigating complex noble politics
  • Assessing the true motives of powerful figures
  • Making decisions that will shape future alliances

Potential Outcomes:

  • Characters align with a noble house
  • Characters make powerful enemies
  • Characters discover a plot against one or more houses

Scene 6: The Keeper's Test

Setting: A secret Keeper facility Overview: The Keepers subject the characters to tests to determine their threat level and potential usefulness.

Key NPCs:

  • Keeper Commander Hayes (Leader of Local Keepers)
  • Initiate Parker (Sympathetic Keeper)
  • The Observer (Mysterious Evaluator)

Challenges:

  • Surviving or evading shadow-based tests
  • Proving their value or innocence
  • Learning about the Keepers' true purpose

Potential Outcomes:

  • Characters gain Keeper allies or enemies
  • Characters learn about forbidden shadow techniques
  • Characters discover critical information about current tensions

Act 3: Shadows Cast

Scene 7: Uncovering the Plot

Setting: Various locations following leads Overview: Characters piece together the fragments they've gathered to uncover a major threat to Ravenshire.

Key NPCs:

  • Conspirators (Various)
  • Informants (Various)
  • Potential Allies (Various)

Challenges:

  • Connecting disparate pieces of information
  • Determining who can be trusted
  • Moving quickly as events accelerate

Potential Outcomes:

  • Characters identify the nature of the threat
  • Characters form a plan to address the threat
  • Characters solidify their alliances

Scene 8: The Shadow Conflict

Setting: Dramatic location related to the threat Overview: Characters confront the main antagonists and work to prevent or mitigate the threat.

Key NPCs:

  • Primary Antagonist
  • Allied NPCs
  • Innocent Bystanders

Challenges:

  • Facing powerful shadow abilities
  • Protecting themselves and others
  • Making difficult moral choices

Potential Outcomes:

  • The threat is neutralized, diminished, or succeeds
  • Characters demonstrate their values through choices
  • The balance of power in Ravenshire shifts

Scene 9: New Shadows

Setting: Aftermath locations around Ravenshire Overview: Characters deal with the consequences of the conflict and establish their new place in Ravenshire.

Key NPCs:

  • Surviving allies and enemies
  • New power figures
  • Citizens affected by events

Challenges:

  • Adapting to the new status quo
  • Managing their reputation and relationships
  • Deciding their future involvement with shadow society

Potential Outcomes:

  • Characters gain a permanent place in Ravenshire society
  • The Shadow Pact is maintained, reformed, or fractured
  • Seeds are planted for future adventures

Adaptation Guidance

Pacing

This structure is designed for approximately 6-9 sessions of play, but can be condensed or expanded:

  • For a shorter adventure, combine scenes within each act
  • For a longer campaign, expand each scene and add additional complications
  • Allow pacing to respond to player engagement with specific story elements

Player Agency

The outcomes listed are possibilities, not requirements:

  • Let character decisions genuinely affect the direction of the story
  • Be prepared to improvise new connections if players take unexpected approaches
  • Focus on consistent characterization of NPCs rather than forcing specific outcomes

Tone Adjustment

The default tone is mysterious and dramatic, but you can adjust:

  • For more action, emphasize confrontations with shadow powers
  • For more intrigue, focus on the complex web of noble relationships
  • For more horror, highlight the disturbing aspects of shadow manipulation
  • For more heroism, give characters clear opportunities to protect innocents
