Narrono Game Manual

Shadow Court

Welcome to Shadow Court, a complete adventure module for the Narrono roleplaying system. This module provides everything you need to run a mysterious and dramatic story in the city of Ravenshire, where ancient noble families secretly wield supernatural powers.

Overview

In Shadow Court, players take on the roles of individuals who discover and become entangled in the supernatural politics of Ravenshire. As they uncover the truth about the city's noble families and their shadow powers, they'll navigate complex relationships, face dangerous opponents, and make decisions that will shape the city's future.

Module Contents

  • Setting: The world of Ravenshire and its hidden supernatural elements
  • Story: A structured adventure framework with scenes and challenges
  • Characters: Pre-generated characters ready to play

Themes

Shadow Court explores several core themes:

  • Power and responsibility
  • Secrets and their consequences
  • Personal ambition versus collective good
  • Tradition versus progress
  • The nature of justice

How to Use This Module

For Game Facilitators

  1. Read the Setting document to understand the world of Ravenshire
  2. Familiarize yourself with the pre-generated characters and their connections
  3. Review the Story framework to understand the narrative structure
  4. Prepare any additional notes or adaptations suited to your group
  5. Guide your players through character selection or creation
  6. Begin with the introductory scene described in the Story document

For Players

  1. Read the Setting overview provided by your Game Facilitator
  2. Choose one of the pre-generated characters or create your own with GF approval
  3. Consider your character's relationships with other player characters
  4. Think about your character's motivations regarding the Shadow Court

Adventure Hooks

Here are a few ways to draw characters into the adventure:

  • A mysterious attack with supernatural elements
  • An invitation to an exclusive social event with hidden agendas
  • The disappearance of someone connected to one of the characters
  • Discovery of an ancient artifact with ties to shadow magic
  • Witnessing something that cannot be explained by normal means

Customization Options

While Shadow Court works as a complete adventure out of the box, feel free to adapt it to your group's preferences:

  • Adjust the power level of shadow abilities
  • Modify relationships between characters or families
  • Add or remove faction elements
  • Change the city's details to match your preferred setting
  • Expand or contract the timeline of events

Enjoy your journey into the shadows of Ravenshire!
